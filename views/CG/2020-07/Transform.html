# Rotation Matrix(Rodrigues' Rotation Formula)

R(n,α)=cos(α)I+(1−cos(α))nnT+sin(α)(0−nznynz0−nx−nynx0) R(n, \alpha) = cos(\alpha)I + (1-cos(\alpha))nn^T+sin(\alpha)\begin{pmatrix} 0 & -n_z & n_y \\ n_z & 0 & -n_x \\ -n_y & n_x & 0 \end{pmatrix} R(n,α)=cos(α)I+(1−cos(α))nnT+sin(α)⎝⎜⎛​0nz​−ny​​−nz​0nx​​ny​−nx​0​⎠⎟⎞​
